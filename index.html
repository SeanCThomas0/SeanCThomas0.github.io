Kald

in progress

A location-based prediction market with native iOS and Android apps. Most of the interesting work is in making it hard to cheat:

  • Identity is tied to device-bound, non-exportable ECDSA P-256 keys — every action is a signed request with server-issued nonces and replay protection.
  • Money movement lives entirely in SECURITY DEFINER Postgres functions with row-level locking and idempotency keys; RLS on every table.
  • Outcomes settle through a trust-weighted multi-resolver consensus engine, with signed disputes and anti-snipe hold windows, fed by GPS and device-integrity evidence.
